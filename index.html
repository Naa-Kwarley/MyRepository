Site A Homes

The Phase 4 – Site A Homes are a proposed thirty (30) unit up-market residential haven of three (3) bedroom luxurious storey buildings. The project is situated on a 10,317m2 parcel of land opposite the City of Favour Church and our Phase 3 development. The houses have been set in a layout that allows for considerable space and ease of movement for both humans and vehicles. Distinct to all our housing developments, this estate is also a gated community with a 24-hour security service.

For Your Comfort

The residential facility shall come with amenities including but not limited to a water reservoir, well trained security personnel and modern security technologies, concierge, and a common area management services plus other features such as fitted water tanks and pumps, security doors, paved walkways, fibre optic cabling network (WiFi), lightning protection, kitchenette in master suite, fitted kitchen with cabinets, stove/oven, microwave, extractor and washing machine connection point.

Hillside Homes

The Hillside Homes are plush detached four-bedroom townhouses carefully designed and built on a hill for your habitation. The development is designed to be the pacesetter energy efficient residential hub in Ghana. The project seeks to combine architectural finesse with style concealed in a refined yet classy manner. These homes will avail prospective clients with a top-of-the-range distinct slice of Accra’s luxury residential experience; while offering potential buyers an investment to cherish forever.

Keys to Success

The local residential construction market is upsurging at the moment. To achieve an incontrovertible position in this environment, the Phase 4 project executor seeks to concentrate on the following;

  • Completing renderings and other architectural designs on the project within the stipulated work schedule
  • Beginning an aggressive off-plan marketing to lock in buyers within the upper end segment of
    the real estate market.
