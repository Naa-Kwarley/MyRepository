Hillside Homes

These are a set of fifteen (15) houses that will be constructed behind the Achimota New Station and in close proximity to the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Authority (DVLA). The Hillside Homes are plush detached four-bedroom townhouses carefully designed and built on a hill for your habitation. Our homes are situated on a land size of at least 45 x 80 feet. Considerable time and energy have been expended to meet the details and specifications outlined by our architect and engineers. The development is designed to be the pacesetter energy efficient residential hub in Ghana. With a clean and pristine outlook, the project seeks to combine architectural finesse with style concealed in a refined yet classy manner.

There will be another twelve (12) houses that will be fashioned opposite the Hillside Homes. These houses are deluxe three bedroom storey buildings with a touch of class, elegance and professional finishing works. It is set on a land size of about 5889m2.

The final set of thirteen (13) houses is designed to be constructed on the northern terminus of the eco-park. The development here will be a baroque three bedroom detached basement houses erected on a 5209m2.

Our finished houses come clean with a fitted kitchen that has a microwave, oven, stove and an extractor.
