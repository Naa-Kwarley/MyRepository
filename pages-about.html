About The Phase 4 Project

The Phase 4 Project embodies the fourth phase of the multiple projects undertaken by JL Properties in Achimota. It is a transformational agenda which was started about six (6) years ago by the visionary Executive Chairman, Dr James Orleans-Lindsay.

To him, spending his own resources to reclaim and turn 12-acres of landfill site into a green zone is his gratuitous contribution to societal progress. His goal is to make Achimota the most preferred hub in the capital, thereby, building plush estates/gated community in the area that makes life exciting and animating. The Phase 4 Project is a three-fold project that is being embarked upon to enliven activities in the Achimota area while affording the community a typical picturesque view of an evolving burghal.

Mission

The Phase 4 Project seeks to offer premium quality and efficient designs, the finest of fittings, and first rate management services all enveloped in a residential facility compromising a recreational centre, townhouses and apartments set within a landscape that provides a soothing and superior sense of living.

Objectives

The Phase 4 Project is planned to:

  • Develop a system of steady green projects that fantastically draws individuals to nature.
  • Undertake a quality yet cost efficient project that brings increased margins to prospective home owners and other key stakeholders.
  • Construct a landmark residential facility within the Golf Hills vicinity that makes a distinct statement of lifestyle, finesse and luxury - all rolled in one.
  • Include green and energy efficient technologies in the development to enhance environmental sustainability and reduce utility costs of potential residents.
  • Provide the greater part of the city with a safe, fun, exciting (and profitable) entertainment facility for groups and families, which will help individuals grow physically, emotionally and mentally through interactive play with guardians, peers and the facility itself.
