Site A Homes

Site A Homes are a proposed thirty (30) unit up-market residential haven of three (3) bedroom luxurious storey buildings. The project is situated on a 10,317m2 parcel of land opposite the City of Favour Church and our Phase 3 development. It is a minute walk from the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Authority (DVLA) office at Achimota on the by-pass road leading to Christian Village. The houses have been set in a layout that allows for considerable space and ease of movement for both humans and vehicles. All residential homes are detached. Distinct to all our housing developments, this estate is also a gated community with a 24-hour security service.

All the 70 houses are ensuite and tiled with either Italian or Spanish (porcelain, ceramic, marble, granite, travertine, woodlook, slate, pebbles & stones, onyx or quartzite) tiles to give a solid finishing. We add a foreign taste to our finishing by using security doors from Turkey and vinyl silk paint from England. The paints are customized to withstand the harsh weather conditions typical to Ghana. Unique to all our homes are the matchless pergola woodwork that serves as a garage to safeguard at least two (2) cars and the stone pitching driveways.
