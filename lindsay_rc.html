Lindsay Recreational Centre

Recreation is an essential part of human life and finds many different forms which are shaped naturally by individual interests as well as the surrounding social construction.

Lindsay Recreational Centre (L.R.C) is a recreational facility designed for either communal or individual use. We have both indoor and outdoor facilities depending on one’s preference. A significant section of our recreational centre is designated for hobbies which are activities done for pleasure on a regular basis. Most people spend their time in activities of daily living, work, sleep, social duties, and leisure, the latter time being free from prior commitments to physiological or social needs. L.R.C is characteristically established to offer individuals a place to undertake quality leisure. A time not consumed by the necessities of living, but to afford individuals the opportunity to consider and reflect on the values and realities that are missed in the activities of daily life, thus being an essential element of personal development.

About Lindsay Recreational Centre

L.R.C is an eight (8) acre space set aside for family zest and recreation on the one side and specialised groups on the other. It is landscaped with burgeoning turf and grassy areas, meticulously aligned trees planted and given topiary to reveal heaven on earth. Strikingly featured is a resplendent multi-functional 5,000 seater pavilion (dome) for commercial use, a swimming pool, a gymnasium and some enlivening playground structures for children. L.R.C. has an astro-turf soccer pitch, basketball & tennis courts, plus paved areas for your leisure and pleasure. In addition, there are also the immaculate trails for walking, biking and other activities. Withal, a number of spiffy garden chairs, picnic tables and barbecue grills have been strategically set out for your utmost benefit.

L.R.C is an adult supervised play center that offers a series of activities devoted to the physical, emotional and social development of individuals. We offer a large, full scale play structure for kids and a smaller play structure for toddlers. L.R.C offers families and groups in the city and the surrounding areas a superior family/group recreation center where all equipment is safety-tested and of the highest quality while ensuring that employees are trained in child safety and first aid delivery. Moreover, parents will have special seating areas where they can watch their children play actively and safely, and can purchase snacks and drinks from the facility.

There is no facility dedicated exclusively to building the social, physical, and mental interactions of individuals in an exciting and stimulating environment, and L.R.C seeks to fill this void.

The Lindsay Recreational Centre is available for general public use. The centre is open to the public seven days a week. Our Park and Recreation staff are dedicated to courtly service to enhance the quality of life for community residents, workers and visitors.
